A Single Vision to Transform Lives

The journey of Basti Ki Pathshala Foundation began not in a boardroom, but in the heart of one individual. It all began when our founder, Sunita, inspired by her own experiences and driven by a deep-seated passion for social justice, embarked on a mission to address the stark educational inequalities she witnessed in underserved communities.

Armed with determination and fueled by compassion, she rallied a team of like-minded individuals who shared her vision of a brighter, more equitable future. From these humble beginnings, our organization has grown into a beacon of hope, touching the lives of countless children and families along the way.
